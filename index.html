I have a lot of different hobbies that I enjoy. One passion I've always had is sports. Since I was a little kid I've always loved playing and watching all kinds of sports. Nowadays I mostly just play recreational basketball, football and soccer with my friends. I'm a big Philadelphia sports fan and I watch every Eagles, Sixers and Flyers game I can. Because of my interest in sports, one of my career goals is to become an Athletic Director for a highschool or college. Another interest I have is nature. I love to go hiking and kayaking with friends, and to explore new places. I enjoy fishing a lot as well, but mostly just for the outdoors aspect of it. One of my newer interests is contributing to political and social change. Over the past few years I have become very passionate about many of the issues that people in this country and across the world are facing, and I want to do what I can to help people. This includes doing volunteer work and joining organizations that promote political and social change.

This class was my introduction to web development and coding. Before this class I had always been interested in learning how coding worked, but it all seemed so difficult and overwhelming that I never tried. I'm glad to say that I actually found it to be much easier than I was expecting and I learned a lot in this class. Even if I never pursure a career that is specific to web development, I think a lot of what I learned could be useful knowledge in other areas, and I'm glad that I took this class and now have a general understanding of the basics.
